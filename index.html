Panela

How to Play
  1. 2-5 teams, 1-3 players each
  2. Set the timer and words per player
  3. Enter all words for the game
  4. Teams take turns: one speaker helps their team guess
  5. A round ends when all words are used

Rounds

  • Round 1: Explain freely (not the word itself)
  • Round 2: Use only one word
  • Round 3: Use only charades

The team that guesses the most words wins!
